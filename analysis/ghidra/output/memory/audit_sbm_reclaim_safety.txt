AUDIT: old SBM reclaim safety for gheap VAS pressure
======================================================================

heap/arena reservations after hooks are active. Do not assume safety:
pre-hook allocations may still be freed through original GameHeap, and
unhooked internal SBM paths may still touch arena freelists.
