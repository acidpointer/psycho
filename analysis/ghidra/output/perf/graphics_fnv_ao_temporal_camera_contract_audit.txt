FNV AO TEMPORAL CAMERA CONTRACT AUDIT

Questions to prove before temporal AO implementation:
1. Does WorldSceneGraph + 0xAC own the camera matching world depth at image-space time?
2. Are NiAVObject world rotation +0x68..+0x88 and translation +0x8C..+0x94 current and stable there?
3. Which columns are forward, up, and right, and what signs map positive view X/Y/Z to world space?
4. How do native camera-relative transform consumers multiply the basis and translation?
5. Is the world camera restored after Main::RenderFirstPerson and before ProcessImageSpaceShaders?
6. Can OMV safely retain one copied transform across Present as previous-frame history?

Required outcome: an explicit current-view -> world -> previous-view formula with no inferred offsets or handedness.
