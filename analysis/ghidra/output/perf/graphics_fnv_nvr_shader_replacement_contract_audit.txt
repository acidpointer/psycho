FNV NVR SHADER REPLACEMENT CONTRACT AUDIT

Questions:
1. What exact ABI do CreateVertexShader/CreatePixelShader expose in FNV?
2. Does SetShaders read current NiD3DPass and current geometry from stable globals?
3. Which shader singleton arrays are safe to inspect without mutating engine objects?
4. What allocation-size patch sites did NVR rely on, and should Psycho avoid them?

Psycho compatibility rule:
Use side tables keyed by NiD3DVertexShader/NiD3DPixelShader pointers. Do not extend native object layouts unless a separate opt-in mode proves ownership and conflicts.
