1. What exact object/vector does vanilla use for sun screen projection?
2. Which transform and visibility helper generate native sun screen coordinates?
3. What is the world -> first-person -> image-space render order?
4. Which depth surface/render-target state is active at each OMV hook boundary?
5. Where is first-person depth cleared and where do offscreen target transitions occur?
6. Where are camera near/far and projection globals updated?
7. Which sites collide with DepthResolve-style ownership?
