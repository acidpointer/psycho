Bridge contract questions
======================================================================

2. At every E7EB00 call, what is ECX, what is EDI, and do those registers carry record +4/+8 state?
3. Does any bridge caller retain a pointer back to the BA9EE0 entry, its +0x0B layer byte, or the type-3 material property?
4. If the bridge is lossy, native PBR must capture material arrays before the bridge and key by a proven owner/entry generation.
