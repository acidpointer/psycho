WEATHER/FOG CONTRACT TO PROVE
======================================================================
Read-only default runtime is acceptable only after these are proven in FNV:
  Sky firstWeather, secondWeather, weatherPercent offsets.
  TESWeather day/night fog near/far fields or getter functions.
  Fog color/current fog blend source used by image-space/fog rendering.
  Interior/exterior and weather transition behavior at image-space phase.

Do not implement fog-aware AO or godray weather constants from TESReloaded offsets alone.
